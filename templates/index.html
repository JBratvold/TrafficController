Traffic Controls

About this Neural Network

This neural network is designed to optimize traffic light control at an intersection. By analyzing data such as the number of cars waiting at red and green lights, and the time since the last light change, the model predicts the best traffic light state to improve traffic flow. The goal is to reduce congestion, minimize wait times, and enhance overall traffic efficiency. The model was trained using historical traffic data, and its predictions are made in real-time as new data is provided by sensors at the intersection.
