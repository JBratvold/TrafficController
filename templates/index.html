About the Traffic-Controller System

Overview

This AI-driven system optimizes traffic light timing to improve traffic flow at intersections. By analyzing real-time data, such as the number of cars waiting at signals and the time elapsed since the last light change, it reduces congestion and enhances traffic efficiency.

How It Works

The system collects real-time data from traffic sensors at the intersection and uses a neural network to predict whether or not the traffic lights should change, based on the training data it learned from. By continuously analyzing current traffic conditions, the AI dynamically adjusts the traffic light to optimize flow, reduce congestion, and adapt to changing traffic patterns.

Note: To minimize network strain and ensure optimal system performance, API calls to the neural network are made every 5 seconds.

AI Training and Learning

The AI system is trained using a dataset of historical traffic patterns. During training, the neural network employs Supervised Learning with Binary Classification to understand how different traffic conditions influence optimal light timings. The model uses activation functions like ReLU for hidden layers and Sigmoid for the output layer, enabling it to make binary predictions. The training process also uses Binary Cross-Entropy as the loss function to optimize the model's performance. Once trained, the model is used to predict whether or not to change the light, based on real-time traffic data.

How Cars Spawn and Move

Cars are randomly generated at the edges of the intersection and move according to the traffic light signals. You can also spawn a car using the manual controls. If a car enters the intersection just as the light turns red, it will accelerate to exit the intersection faster.

Manual Controls

  • Change Light: Click to cycle through the red and green lights, manually controlling traffic flow.
  • Add Car: Click to spawn a new car at a random edge of the intersection.
  • Pause/Resume: Pause or resume the simulation to observe traffic flow changes.

Key Benefits

  • Reduced Congestion: Shorter wait times at red lights, improving traffic flow.
  • Smoother Driving: Fewer stop-and-go situations, resulting in a more pleasant driving experience.
  • Adaptive to Traffic Fluctuations: Instant adjustments to changing traffic conditions for maximum efficiency.
  • Environmental Impact: Optimized traffic management reduces fuel consumption and emissions.

As the AI continues to learn from each data point, the system becomes smarter, driving even more efficient and adaptive traffic control, transforming how intersections are managed.
